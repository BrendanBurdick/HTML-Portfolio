Opening Statements!

® Uber Café, located in Mendon, Massachusetts, offers a great selection of premium coffees, teas, and espresso for your enjoyment. We also offer fresh baked pastries and treats, as well as a
selection of freshly prepared lunch and dinner items. We pride ourselves on using locally grown ingredients. We only use locally grown eggs from Blackstone Valley Farm and we proudly

About Us!

Our philosophy here at Uber Café is to offer exceptional products for our guests to enjoy. We have something for the whole family- from gourmet coffee and beverages, to delicious breakfast
and lunch items, as well as delightful fresh baked treats. We offer freshly brewed gourmet Red Barn Coffee Roasters coffee along with iced coffee, latte, espresso and a wonderful selection of
teas and flavorful smoothies. Our Breakfast and Lunch menu is made up of familiar items but with a unique twist that makes them deliciously different and exciting. All of our items are freshly
prepared using local and organic ingredients. What’s a great beverage without a tasty treat to enjoy? We freshly bake scrumptious goodies like muffins, cinnamon rolls, cookies, brownies and
cupcakes. We bake daily so our selection is constantly changing and always fresh and delicious. When you visit, you can enjoy your tasty treat in our shop or on our patio along with free Wi-Fi.
If you are in a hurry, take your treat to go. Either way, stop on by and enjoy! It’s uberlicious! (Taken from the official website)
