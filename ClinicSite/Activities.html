Activities!

Check out some of the activities we have planned:


Practices

We will host practices about two or three times per week. During practices, we will work on a variety of different categories. The main baseball focus during practices will be building strength and endurance, and mastering fundamentals.


Offseason Events

During the offseason, we will host a week long camp once a month from December until March. This is in an effort to kick start a strong offseason practice habit to carry over into the new season. This will be a week-long program of intensive practices that will be tailored to need and abilities of each player.


Tournaments

We shall participate in a minimum of two tournaments during the season, with the desire to participate three or four tournaments. This will allow our players and families the opportunity to travel, and more importantly, face competition from around the country. By increasing the level of competition, the players ability to learn also increases.
