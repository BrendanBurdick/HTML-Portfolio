Introduction

The goal of this website is to provide all of the necessary information that anyone would need to fully utilize ® LBS Baseball Clinic. Ever since I was young, I've had a passion for baseball. Now that I'm about to graduate college, I want to take my passion for baseball and turn it into a business where I can share what I've learned! By choosing this clinic, you will be able to hone your skills as a baseball player and visibly see improvement in your game.

This clinic offers a variety of programs that can improve your skills, including:

  • Batting Practice
  • Ground Ball Handling
  • Fly-Ball Handling
  • In-Game Procedures
  • In-Game Strategy
  • Much More!


    • LBS Clinic is partnered with a number of companies in the industry. In 2007, LBS Clinic entered a multi-year partnership with Under Armour, who became the title sponsor for various LBS Clinic and Team One Baseball events. These events include the Under Armour All-America Game, Under Armour LBS Clinic National Tryouts, Under Armour National Teams, Under Armour LBS Clinic National Training Camps and Under Armour Showcases, powered by Team One.

    LBS Clinic is a Premier Scouting Partner for Baseball America, the leading source in the nation for in-depth articles on high school, college, minor league and major league baseball players. LBS Clinic's scouting staff provides Baseball America with detailed scouting reports on the thousands of players seen throughout the year.

    LBS Clinic announced a strategic partnership with "Little League International" in March 2009 in order to provide youth players worldwide with increased player development and college placement opportunities. Little League International named LBS Clinic as the Exclusive and Official Player Development Partner of Little League Baseball.

    LBS Clinic announced a partnership with EyeBlack.com in November 2011. EyeBlack.com, the official EyeBlack of athletes and fans, will have a significant presence at LBS Clinic events.

    Most recently, LBS Clinic announced the formation of a partnership with HeavySwing, the original handle-weighted training bat. As of March 2012, HeavySwing will have a significant presence at all Baseball Factory event's. (Copyright to Wikipedia)
